Tour Guide Overview
Traveling to Vietnam by motorbike is popular nowadays for both Vietnamese and foreigners. Vietnam Motorbike Tour Expert provides the best Vietnam motorcycle tours with well-educated tour guides. All trips at Vietnam Motorbike Tour Expert are guided by one or a few tour guides based on the number of riders in the groups as below:
For small tours that have under 3 riders:
there will only be 1 Tour Guide. This Tour Guide not only works as a regular Tour Guide but also works as a mechanic who can repair and solve technical problems on a long trip. In addition, the tour guide can play the role of a photographer, or a good videographer.
For small tours that have under 3 riders:
Vietnam Motorbike Tour Expert will have to provide 1 Tour Guide and 1 mechanic (mechanic) accompanied.
For small tours that have under 3 riders:
There will be 2 Tour Guides and 1 Mechanic to ensure the best quality of the trip.
The local guide team of Vietnam Motorbike Tour Expert can work not only as professional motorbike tour guides but also cultivate a warm and friendly rapport with the rider guests like close friends. That’s impressing a lot of the riders all over the world who did tour with us and it’s also an important reason many of them come back to do the second and third or fourth time with the same friends – tour guides

1. Ta Xuan Cuong – Johnnie Walker

Johny was born in 1984 in Quang Ninh and has 11 years of experience as a supportive tour guide. In addition to his deep love for Vietnamese culture and history, he possesses a profound understanding of the cultures and customs of each ethnic minority.

His forte lies in offroad motorbike tours; he relishes navigating winding, narrow roads that lead to picturesque and romantic destinations.

Johny never tires of two things in life: riding a motorbike and enjoying a drink. Touring Vietnam with Johny will undoubtedly be a fun-filled adventure!
