THE BEST MOTORCYLES FOR TOURING IN VIETNAM

We are so proud to say up to now (after the COVID-19 pandemic) We are still one of the motorbike tour organizers in Vietnam who have the MOST and the BEST MOTORBIKES for any motorbike tours in Vietnam.
For some reason, most of Vietnamese people ride under 175cc motorbikes or scooters. firstly, the bigger motorbike we have to import and the government charges an import tax of at least 100% with a huge fee for registration so generally it may cost twice as expensive as in a foreign country.( it’s too expensive for most people in Vietnam). Secondly, Vietnamese traffic law allows motorbike’s maximum speed is 40 km/h in the city and 60 or 70km/h on high way. So the bigger bikes seem to be not necessary in Vietnam.
Over the year riding motorbike tours in Vietnam we understood that a high quality bike is one of the most important things to make the perfect motorbike trip.
